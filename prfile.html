About Me

MS Dhoni, full name Mahendra Singh Dhoni, is an Indian cricketer and one of the most successful captains in cricket history. He is known for his calm demeanor under pressure and has led India to numerous victories in international cricket, including the ICC World T20 in 2007 and the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.
